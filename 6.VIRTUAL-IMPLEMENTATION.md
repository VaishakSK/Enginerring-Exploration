# 5. Mechanisms and resource specifications
## Motor sizing and selection
### 1.Motor selection for Door push and pull mechanism

Volume=40cm X 20cm X 1cm = 800 cm^3

mass=density X Volume
    =(0.000961 X 800)kg
    =0.77kg


Force=mass X gravity
     = 0.77 X 9.81
     = 7.54 kg m/s2


Torque= 0.0754 X 10 cm
      = 0.754 kg-cm


Final Torque= Torque X FOS
            = 0.754 X 1.5
            =1.13 kg-cm


Thus, From the above calculations a SG-90 SERVO MOTOR is best suitable for the automatic opening and closing of the door.
***
### 2.Motor Selection for Barrel cam Mechanism
Volume of cylindrical cam= 3.142 X 2.5cm X 2.5cm X 5 cm = 98.1875 cm^3

Volume of fishing hook= 27.9125 cm^3

Total Volume= 98.1875 + 27.9125cm^3 = 126.1 cm^3

mass=126.1 X 0.9 = 119.87 g = 0.119 kg

Force= 0.119 X 9.81= 1.16 kg m/s2

Torque= .016X 5 = 0.08 kg-cm

Final Torque=0.5 X 1.5= 0.12 kg-cm

Thus from the above calculations, a STEPPER MOTOR is best suitable for the barrel cam mechanism
