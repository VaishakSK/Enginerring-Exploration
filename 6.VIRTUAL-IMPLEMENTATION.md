# 5. Mechanisms and resource specifications
## Motor sizing and selection
### 1.Motor selection for Door push and pull mechanism

Volume=45cm X 20cm X 1.5cm = 1350 cm^3

mass=density X Volume
    =(0.000961 X 1350)kg
    =1.31kg


Force=mass X gravity
     = 1.31 X 9.81
     = 12.75 kg m/s2


Torque= 0.1275 X 10 cm
      = 1.275 kg-cm


Final Torque= Torque X FOS
            = 1.275 X 1.5
            =1.9125 kg-cm


Thus, From the above calculations a TOWER PRO MG-995 4.8V SERVO MOTOR is best suitable for the automatic opening and closing of the door.
***
### 2.Motor Selection for Barrel cam Mechanism
Volume of cylindrical cam= 3.142 X 2.5cm X 2.5cm X 5 cm = 98.1875 cm^3

Volume of fishing hook= 27.9125 cm^3

Volume of metal shaft=3.92 cm^3

Total Volume= 98.1875 + 27.9125 + 3.92 cm^3 = 129.92 cm^3

mass=129.92 X 7.85 = 1019.87 g = 1.01 kg

Force= 1.01 X 9.81= 10 kg m/s2

Torque= .10 X 5 = 0.5 kg-cm

Final Torque=0.5 X 1.5= 0.75 kg-cm

Thus from the above calculations, a 300RPM 12V LOW NOISE DC MOTOR WITH METAL GEARS GRADE-A is best suitable for the barrel cam mechanism
